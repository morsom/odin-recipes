Porridge

Ingredients

  • 1.5dl Water
  • 1.5dl Milk
  • 1.5 dl oats
  • Fruits from 1 pomegranate
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Add the water, milk, oats and salt to a caserole or small pot
  2. While stirring, bring to a boil
  3. Turn the heat down to a gentle boil and stir sporadically until the porridge has the desired consistency.
  4. Serve with the pomegranate on top.
