Dhaal

Ingredients

  • Ghee
  • 3 tbsp Turmeric
  • 3 tbsp Ground Cumin
  • 3 tbsp Ground Coreander
  • 5 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Chili
  • 3-4 Onion(s)
  • 200g Red Lentils
  • 100g Green Lentils
  • Small Can Tomato Paste
  • Approximately one liter of hot water

Steps

  1. Chop the onion, garlic and chili
  2. In a large pot, warm up the ghee on medium heat
  3. Add the spices to pot and simmer until a lovely smell appears
  4. Add the onion and fry until soft and sweet, about 5 minutes
  5. Add the chili and garlic and fry for an additional minute
  6. Add the tomato paste and fry it a little
  7. Add The water, boullion and lentils and stir it
  8. Let it simmer for at least half an hour. More won't hurt. Keep an eye that it doesn't become too dry, if so then add more water.
