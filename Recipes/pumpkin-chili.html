Pumpkin Chili

Ingredients

  • 3 cans of beans (black, kidney, black-eyes...) or boils and prepare 4 dl of dried beans
  • 1 Hokkaido pumpkin or similar
  • 1 Large Carrot
  • 1 Red bell pepper
  • 2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 2 tsp Oregano
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Chili
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 tbsb Soy sauce
  • Olive oil

    • To serve
  • Tortilla chips
  • Diced avocado
  • Chopped onion
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the pumpkin in 4 pieces. Remove the innards.
  2. Steam the pumpkin for 20-30 minutes, until soft
  3. Blend the pumpkin to a pure and put aside.
  4. Chop the onion, garlic and chili
  5. In a large pot, warm up the olive oil on medium heat
  6. Add the onion, bell pepper and carrot, and fry for about 5 minutes
  7. Add the chili and garlic and fry for an additional minute
  8. Add the soy sauce, coreander and ground cumin
  9. Now add the beans and pumpkin pure. If it's too thick, add some how water
  10. Bring to a boil and then let it simmer for half an hour or longer
  11. Serve with avocado, chips, onion, hot sauce, cilantro and what else you feel like.
